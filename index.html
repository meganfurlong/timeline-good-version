Jurassic Park

Dinosaur Bones

Alan Grant and Ellie Sattler are two archeologists who are working on a dig and also happen to be dating.

John Hammond shows up on their dig site to request they come to sign off on his new amusement park. In exchange he offers funding for three years. Alan and Ellie happily agree and leave with him to his island.

The Park

Ellie, Alan and John arrive at the park along with Donald Genarra, a lawyer, and Ian Malcolm, a mathematician. Upon landing, the group is greeted by the sight of brachiosauruses roaming about, reavealing the wonder of what John’s scientists have created. Jurassic Park: an island full of dinosaurs once extinct.

“Your scientists were so preoccupied with whether or not they could, they didn’t stop to think if they should.”

Dinosaur Genes

The group heads into the Visitor’s Center where they learn how these scientists have been able to create dinosaurs. They have extracted dinosaur genes from blood inside of mosquitos preserved in chunks of amber.

The dinosaurs are also genetically modified. Gaps are filled with frog genes and all of the DNA is edited to be female to prevent breeding.

Park Tour

Ellie, Malcolm, and Alan set off on an automated park tour, driving past the enclosures of various dinosaurs. They are joined by Tim and Lex, John’s neice and nephew.

meanwhile...

Nedry, the park’s programmer, feels underappreciated by John and the other staff. In retaliation he has made deals under the table to steal dinosaur embryos. To enact this plan, Nedry shuts down the park’s systems, including the dinosaur enclosures.

“Life will not be contained. Life breaks free. It expands to new territories, crashes through barriers, painfully, maybe even dangerously.”

Breaking Free

The tour cars are stopped by the T-Rex enclosure when the power is shut off. With no power in the electric fence, the T-Rex breaks free. Malcolm is injured and Donald, the lawyer, is eaten alive. Alan and the kids are thrown into the enclosure left, mostly, unscathed.

Nature Finds a Way

Alan and the kids are forced to spend the night in a tree in the park. The next morning as they head back to the Visitor’s Center, they discover a nest of eggs. Despite being engineered to be all female, some dinosaurs have switched gender so that they can mate.

meanwhile...

Nedry has taken a selection of dinosaur embryos and is trying to flee from the island. An intense storm causes him to get lost in the forest where he loses the embryos. Then, he encounters a poison spitting dinosaur that kills him.

Power

Ellie and one of the dinosaur handlers head out of safety to find the breakers and get the power back on. The highly dangerous velociraptors have escaped from their nearby enclosure and eat the handler. Ellie manages to get the power on alone and runs back to the Visitor’s Center.

Last Stand

The velociraptors attack Tim and Lex in the kitchen of the Visitor’s Center. They escape to the computer room where they meet up with Ellie and Alan. They continue being pursued until finally, the T-Rex breaks into the Visitor’s Center, fighting off the velociraptors and allowing everyone to escape.

An End

Alan, Ellie, Tim, Lex, Malcolm and John all escape from the island by helicopter. John takes a moment to reminisce before they go, knowing that after 5 years of dedication, this is the end for Jurassic Park.
